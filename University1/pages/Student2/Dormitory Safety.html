ESSENTIAL DORMITORY SAFETY RULES

Follow These Guidelines to Ensure a Safe Stay

Electrical Safety

  • Do not overload power sockets.
  • Unplug electrical devices when not in use.
  • Keep electrical appliances away from water sources.

Fire Safety

  • Never use open flames in the dormitory.
  • Do not block fire exits or fire extinguishers with personal items.
  • Know the location of fire alarms and evacuation routes.

Personal & Property Safety

  • Lock the dorm door when leaving even for a short time.
  • Do not share your dorm key or access code with non-residents.
  • Report suspicious people/activities to dorm management immediately.

Facility Safety

  • Do not tamper with safety equipment.
  • Report broken furniture, windows, or locks via the repair system promptly.
  • Avoid placing heavy items on windowsills or unstable surfaces.
