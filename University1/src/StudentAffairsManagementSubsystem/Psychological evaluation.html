Psychology evaluation

Psychology Teacher's Schedule

Instructions: Over the last 2 weeks, how often have you been bothered by the following problems? (Select one option for each question)

1. Do you have little interest or pleasure in doing things?

2. Do you feel down, depressed or hopeless?

3. Do you feel tired or having little energy?

4. Do you have poor appetite or overeating?

5. Do you feel bad about yourself — or that you are a failure or have let yourself or your family down?

6. Do you have trouble concentrating on things, such as reading the newspaper or watching television?

7. Do you move or speak so slowly that other people could have noticed? Or the opposite — being so fidgety or restless that you have been moving around a lot more than usual?

8. Do you have thoughts that you would be better off dead or of hurting yourself in some way?

9. Do you have trouble falling or staying asleep, or sleeping too much?
